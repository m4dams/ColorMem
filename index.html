Color Sequence Game

Instructions: For Easy the sequence increases with one click each time you get the entire sequence right. For Hard mode there are 6 colors and not only does the sequence increase but the sequence itself changes each time.Maximum achievable points for each round are 100. You can replay the sequence up to 3 times but each time the awarded score for the current round will decrease.

  • 1 replay reduces score to 50
  • 2 replays reduce score to 25
  • 3 replays reduce score to 1
If you are ready to begin press start!
